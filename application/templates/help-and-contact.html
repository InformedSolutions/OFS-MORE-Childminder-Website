Help and contacts

Where you can get help to become a childminder.

Your local council

You can get free help and advice from your local council.

Childcare groups and forums

You can get advice and training from professional childcare groups like:

  • Pacey (Professional Association for Childcare and Early Years)
  • Childminding UK

You could also get advice from other childminders on forums such as Childminding Forum.

Questions about your application

Ofsted
enquiries@ofsted.gov.uk

Help with registering online

If you do not feel confident using digital tools like computers, do ask a friend or family for help.

The other organisations on this page may be able to help as well.

You can also call the Ofsted application team on 0300 123 1231.
