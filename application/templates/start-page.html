Register as a childminder (England)

Register with Ofsted to look after children in your home.

Before you start

You need the following:

  • UK criminal record (DBS) certificates
  • DBS certificates for anyone aged 16 or over in the home dated
  • first aid training
  • childcare training
  • health declaration booklet
  • contact details for 2 references
If you or adults in the home have lived abroad in the last 5 years, you need criminal record certificates from the embassies.

How much it costs

It usually costs £35 to register with Ofsted.

Find out about other costs and renewal.

How long it takes

It should take you 30 minutes to fill in this form.

It usually takes up to 12 weeks to register with Ofsted.
