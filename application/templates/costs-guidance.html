Costs of becoming a childminder

Application fee
Caring only for children aged 0 to 5 £35
Caring only for children aged 5 or older £103
Caring for children of all ages £35

This fee is not refundable.

It costs the same to renew every year.

Criminal record (DBS) checks
Your criminal record (DBS) check £52.10
Checks for adults in your home £52.10 each
Health declaration booklet
GP to fill in and sign booklet £90 (approx)
Training
First aid course that covers the age groups you will look after £60 to £200 (approx)
Childcare training £50 to £200 (approx)
Insurance
Public liability insurance £25 to £100 (approx)
Digital personal details records
Register with ICO if you want to keep digital records of children £35

Your house and car insurance also need to be up to date.
