Documents you need for the visit

The inspector will need to check your identity and other documents when they visit the home.

Document type Examples
Photo ID Passport or driving licence
Proof of address (2 pieces of evidence) Gas, electricity or water bill, council tax bill or letter from government agency
Credit card, bank or mortgage statement
Proof of any name changes Marriage or civil partnership certificate, decree absolute, deed poll
Employment history CV or details of where you have worked
First aid Paediatric first aid certificate
Childcare qualifications Certificates
Criminal record (DBS) checks Certificates for everyone aged 16 or older who lives in the home
Driving documents Driving licences, car insurance and MOT certificate (if driving children around)
