Before you start

How much does it cost?

It costs £{{ ey_fee }} to register, or £{{ cr_fee }} if you are only caring for children over 5.

Criminal record (DBS) checks cost £52.10 each.

First aid courses cost around £100 and you may have to pay for early years training, which costs about £200.

  • Read more about childminder costs
Where can I get help?

You can get free help and advice from your local council.

Membership groups like Pacey provide training, guidance and advice.

You could also get advice from other childminders on forums.

  • Find your council's family information service
  • Pacey (Professional Association for Childcare and Early Years)
  • Childminding Forum
